Before You Begin

Please obtain verbal consent to transcribe this conversation and to generate a note using AI tools. Please ensure your patient understands participation is optional, that opting out will not impact their care, and that they can choose to opt-out at any time.

Standard consent wording:
The veteran was informed of the presence of a listening and transcribing tool during the visit and given the option to opt out and agreed to proceed.
